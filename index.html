My Projects

知的 /Chi-teki (RFID Card Reader)

In response to the growing need for environmental sustainability, I developed a dual-system RFID card reader using C and Python. This innovative solution helps manage chaos within shuttles and enhances user safety.

Empowerlink: Keeping Our Cities Cleaner and Greener(NGO)

In a rapidly changing world that often neglects environmental health, pollution continues to rise. Empowerlink is an NGO dedicated to reducing pollution and keeping our streets tidy. I designed this website using HTML and CSS to promote our mission.

Venue Booking Website

The festive season brings numerous fun events and activities, many of which require suitable venues. However, many event management businesses struggle to effectively track their available spaces. This website allows users to select their desired venue and enter their card details for booking. Users can browse venue images and view seating capacities, streamlining the event planning process. I used HTML and CSS for this website.
